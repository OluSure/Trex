Who We Are

We are a team #Act4Action set out to bring excitement to the elderly by educating them on maximising tech tools and gadgets to their fullest possibility.
Regardless of the differences among us we are looking forword to sharing knowledge in health, social and financial technologies to educate the older people about them.

Why We Act

With the high integration of the Internet of Things and the advancement of Aritificial Intelligence the older age group has been far from it, especially in remote areas of Africa where an estimate of 3 million older aged group have not been introduced to massively take advantages of technology tools and gadgets.
Most of the aged population are overwhelmed, intimidated, or excluded from the use of technology due to lack of exposure or traning.

What We Do

We the Trex team are on a mission with excitement to engage the elderly, aged 65 and above on maximising tech tools and gadgets to reduce and control health risks, provide oppourtunities for socialization and connection to eliminate feelings of isolation and loneliness, increase their access to information and resources, and provide better oppourtunity after retirement.
Our goal is to educate 1000 age group 65+ on maximising tech tools and gadgets on the first year of launching this project for Africans.

Another Oppourtunity For The Aged Population In Africa


The SDG 10 (Sustainable Development Goal 10) aims to reduce inequality within and among countries.

In Africa, there are significant challenges related to income inequality, social lifestyle, discrimination, and access to education which can be addressed through the use of technology.

With the high integration of the Internet of Things and the advancement of Artificial Intelligence, the older age group has been far from it, especially in remote areas of Africa where most have not been introduced to massively taking advantage of technology to serve as a means to help manage their health, companion when alone, engage in a better opportunity after retirements.

In Africa, an estimate of 3 million older ages have been experiencing great limitations with the use of technology tools and gadgets. Yet, no significant initiative has been introduced to meet it.

Most of the Elderly in Africa are overwhelmed, intimidated, or excluded from the use of technology due to lack of exposure or training.

The Trex team are people who believe that most people are missing out on utilising tech tools and gadgets to their fullest potential when they become aged.

We want to grow older with a meaningful purpose by taking as many people in the world along as the Trex team on enlightening the aged on tech tools and gadgets we will take it as a responsibility to see that we bring excitement to the elderly by educating them on maximising tech tools and gadgets to their fullest possibility.

Our well planned educative outreach event is set to hold in different countries in Africa to engage the elderly on the use of common home health tech products and services, popular social applications and profitable financial services which we believe will help them be confident in using them or teaching others on how to use whenever they needed them or get in contact with one.

We hope to do exceedingly more together with you in other areas as we have planned to reach 1000 elderly in the first year of our launching in Africa as a whole to maximise tech tools and gadgets to their fullest potential.

Thank you.
