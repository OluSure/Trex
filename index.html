    Another Oppourtunity For The Population In Africa


    The SDG 10 (Sustainable Development Goal 10) aims to reduce inequality within and among countries.

    In Africa, there are significant challenges related to income inequality, social lifestyle, discrimination, and access to education which can be addressed through the use of technology.

    With the high integration of the Internet of Things and the advancement of Artificial Intelligence, the older age group has been far from it, especially in remote areas of Africa where most have not been introduced to massively taking advantage of technology to serve as a means to help manage their health, companion when alone, engage in a better opportunity after retirements.

    In Africa, over 3 million older ages have been experiencing great limitations with the use of technology tools and gadgets. Yet, no significant initiative has been introduced to meet it.

    Most of the Elderly in Africa are overwhelmed, intimidated, or excluded from the use of technology due to lack of exposure or training.

    The Trex team are people who believe that most people are missing out on utilising tech tools and gadgets to their fullest potential when they become aged.

    We want to grow older with a meaningful purpose by taking as many people in the world along as the Trex team on enlightening the population on tech tools and gadgets we will take it as a responsibility to see that we bring excitement to people by educating them on maximising tech tools and gadgets to their fullest possibility.

    Our well planned educative outreach event is set to engage the elderly on the use of common home health tech products and services, popular social applications and profitable financial services which we believe will help them be confident in using them or teaching others on how to use whenever they needed them or get in contact with one.

    We hope to do exceedingly more together with you in other areas as we have planned to reach 1000 people in the first year of our launching in Africa as a whole to maximise tech tools and gadgets to their fullest potential.

    Thank you.
